### [1] [Interaction mechanics of acoustic cavitation with fibrin networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29020)
*Aarushi Bhargava,Gaurav Gardi,Metin Sitti*

Main category: cond-mat.soft

TL;DR: 本研究发现，超声驱动微泡的稳定周期振荡能够穿透并改变僵硬的纤维蛋白网络，通过在网络中创建三维通道，使药物输运示踪纳米珠渗透深度达200微米。这一过程源于气泡径向振荡对纤维蛋白的反复亚断裂加载，诱导塑性变形和损伤累积，而非单次断裂。网络软化行为依赖于施加力的强度：低力下出现"安定效应"（shakedown effect），高力下则持续损伤累积。该发现建立了气泡-纤维蛋白相互作用的黏塑性力学框架，为纤维化陈旧血凝块的治疗策略设计提供了机理基础。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 慢性血凝块中僵硬致密的纤维蛋白网络阻碍药物穿透及分布至凝块核心，限制了溶栓疗法的效果。微泡声空化虽被视为增强软组织药物递送的有效策略，但气泡与僵硬纤维蛋白网络的相互作用机制尚不明确，因此亟需探究这一相互作用过程。

Method: 利用超声驱动微泡产生稳定的周期振荡，观察气泡与纤维蛋白网络的相互作用；结合力学测量分析气泡径向应力对纤维蛋白纤维的作用；在不同峰值作用力下考察纤维蛋白网络的软化行为。

Result: 稳定振荡的气泡能穿透并改变致密纤维蛋白网络，形成三维路径使纳米珠渗透深度达200微米。气泡径向振荡是作用于纤维蛋白纤维的主导驱动机理；单次周期中径向应力不足以断裂纤维，但反复亚断裂加载导致塑性变形与损伤累积。低力下网络出现安定效应，高力下则持续软化且无法达到稳定状态。

Conclusion: 声空化能够增强致密纤维网络中的基质输运，其物理机制由气泡-纤维蛋白相互作用的黏塑性力学（viscoplastic mechanics）主导。该发现建立了机理框架，可用于设计针对纤维化陈旧血凝块的综合治疗策略。

Abstract: Stiff and dense fibrin networks in chronic blood clots impede drug penetration and distribution into the clot core, limiting the efficacy of thrombolytic therapies. Acoustic cavitation of microbubbles is a promising strategy to enhance drug delivery in soft tissues. However, the interaction of these bubbles with stiff fibrin networks has yet to be investigated. Here, we show that ultrasound-driven bubbles undergoing stable periodic oscillations can penetrate and alter dense fibrin networks. The penetrated bubbles create three-dimensional paths that enable nanobeads (matrix transport markers) to infiltrate up to 200 $μ$m m deep into the mesh. Radial bubble oscillation is found to be the dominant forcing mechanism on fibrin fibers. Combining mechanical measurements with these observations reveals that the bubble radial stress is insufficient to break the fibrin fibers in a single cycle. Instead, repeated sub-fracture loading from bubble oscillations induce plastic deformation and damage accumulation with each cycle. This is evident from drastic dissipation losses and softening of the network seen over thousands of cycles. We further explored the softening of fibrin networks at a range of peak applied forces. At low force, the fibrin networks undergo a shakedown effect with initial softening, which is resistant to further damage after hundreds of cycles. At higher force, networks continue to soften without reaching a stable state, indicating progressive damage accumulation. These results show that cavitation can enhance matrix transport in dense fiber networks. The underlying physics is governed by the viscoplastic mechanics of bubble-fibrin interactions. These findings establish a mechanistic framework to design comprehensive treatment strategies for fibrotic aged clots.

### [2] [Self-Assembly of Lipid-Biopolymer Periodic Nanostructures on Photonic Length Scales](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29040)
*Rushna Quddus,Meron Debas,Stefan Salentinig,Ullrich Steiner,Viola Vogler-Neuling*

Main category: cond-mat.soft

TL;DR: 本文研究了昆虫中脂质与生物聚合物的共组装机制，发现脂质介晶相（lyotropic liquid crystal mesophases）可作为模板引导生物聚合物自组装，生成周期在700 nm至1.2 μm的周期性纳米结构（超越纯脂质系统68 nm的限制），为重构生物光子结构色（biophotonic structural colour）的物理化学机制提供了方法基础。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 当前合成脂质自组装系统的周期被限制在68 nm，而自然界（如昆虫）中的光子结构（photonic nanostructures）所需的特征尺度约为450 nm。该差距促使研究者探索脂质是否在体内通过模板作用参与结构色（structural colour）的产生。

Method: 采用动态光散射（dynamic light scattering）、X射线散射（X-ray scattering）和Zeta电位分析（zeta potential analysis）研究生物聚合物、pH、温度、表面电荷和稳定性对脂质囊泡（lipid vesicles）的影响；利用低温电子显微镜（cryo-electron microscopy）观察脂质与生物聚合物相互作用后形成的纳米结构。

Result: 脂质囊泡与生物聚合物相互作用后，生成了周期在700 nm至1.2 μm（比纯脂质系统大10倍以上）、维度从一维到三维（1D to 3D）的周期性纳米结构。

Conclusion: 脂质介晶相与生物聚合物可协同诱导有序纳米结构的重组织，突破了纯脂质系统在周期尺度上的关键限制，为在体外重现生物光子结构色的物理化学机制提供了可行方法。

Abstract: The self-assembly of photonic nanostructures in insects involves chitin, proteins, and lipids. While synthetic photonic systems have been extensively studied, current lipid-based self-assembly systems are limited in periodicity to $68\,\text{nm}$ compared to photonic length scales ($\approx 450\,\text{nm}$) observed in biological organisms. We hypothesise that lipids facilitate how structural colour arises in vivo by acting as templates for the self-assembly of biopolymers via lipidic lyotropic liquid crystal mesophases. Here, we aim to understand and identify how structural colour is produced in insects by the co-assembly of lipids and biopolymers. We study the effect of biopolymers, pH, temperature, surface charge, and stability on lipid vesicles using dynamic light scattering, X-ray scattering, and zeta potential analysis. Using cryo-electron microscopy, we demonstrate that these vesicles interact with the biopolymers and generate periodic nanostructures with periodicities ranging from $700\,\text{nm}$ to $1.2\,μ\text{m}$ (more than ten times larger than for purely lipidic systems) and dimensionalities ranging from 1D to 3D. Our results establish that lipid mesophases and biopolymers can induce reorganisation into ordered nanostructures, overcoming key limitations of periodicities achieved by lipid-only systems, and providing a methodology for recreating the physicochemical mechanisms underlying biophotonic structural colour.

### [3] [Characterization of Spurious Charge in SENSEI Skipper-CCDs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30281)
*Yikai Wu,Ansh Desai,Sho Uemura,Ana M. Botti,Brenda A. Cervantes-Vergara,Fernando Chierchie,Alex Drlica-Wagner,Rouven Essig,Juan Estrada,Erez Etzion,Guillermo Fernandez Moroni,Miqueas Gamero,Stephen E. Holland,Ian Lawson,Steffon Luoma,Nathan A. Saffold,Miguel Sofo-Haro,Javier Tiffenberg,Tomer Volansky*

Main category: physics.ins-det

TL;DR: 本文研究了Skipper电荷耦合器件（Skipper-CCDs）中“杂散电荷”（spurious charge）这一关键背景来源，发现其主要产生于序列寄存器（serial register）在Skipper读出期间的水平时钟保持恒定电压阶段。基于该发现，提出了一种“三电平”（tri-level）时钟方案，通过在读出期间将低电平相提升至中间电压来抑制陷阱介导的电荷生成。在SENSEI探测器中实测表明，该方案将序列寄存器单电子密度从$(2.9 \pm 0.1) \times 10^{-5}$电子/像素/图像降低至$(4.0 \pm 0.4) \times 10^{-6}$电子/像素/图像，实现了约7倍的背景抑制。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Skipper-CCDs是亚GeV暗物质（sub-GeV dark matter）和相干弹性中微子-原子核散射（coherent elastic neutrino-nucleus scattering）探测中的领先技术。其稀有事件搜索的关键背景来源于“杂散电荷”（spurious charge），即电荷在通过有源区（active region）和序列寄存器（serial register）转移至读出级时产生的单电子事件。因此，迫切需要对该背景进行表征并提出有效的抑制方法。

Method: 首先对SENSEI Skipper-CCDs在有源区和序列寄存器中的杂散电荷进行了表征，发现良好屏蔽的低背景环境中，主要贡献来自Skipper读出期间序列寄存器中水平时钟在像素转移间保持恒定电压的阶段。基于此，提出了一种“三电平”（tri-level）时钟方案，将保持低电平相在读出期间提升至中间电压，以抑制陷阱介导的电荷生成（trap-mediated charge generation）。实验测量在MINOS洞穴附近的SENSEI探测器上进行。

Result: 标准SENSEI读出条件下，序列寄存器单电子密度为$(2.9 \pm 0.1) \times 10^{-5}$电子/像素/图像；采用三电平时钟方案后，降低至$(4.0 \pm 0.4) \times 10^{-6}$电子/像素/图像，实现了约7倍的背景抑制。

Conclusion: 三电平时钟方案能够有效抑制Skipper-CCDs中由序列寄存器引起的杂散电荷背景，为当前和未来Skipper-CCD实验降低本底提供了有前景的技术路径。

Abstract: Skipper Charge-Coupled Devices (Skipper-CCDs) are a leading technology in the search for sub-GeV dark matter and coherent elastic neutrino-nucleus scattering. A key background for rare-event searches with these detectors arises from "spurious charge" -- single-electron events generated when charges are transferred through the active region to the serial register, and across the serial register to the readout stage. We present a characterization of spurious charge in both the active region and the serial register of SENSEI Skipper-CCDs, and show that, in a well-shielded low-background environment, the dominant contribution originates in the serial register during Skipper readout, when horizontal clocks are held at constant voltage between pixel transfers. Motivated by this finding, we develop a "tri-level" clocking scheme in which the held-low phase is raised to an intermediate voltage during readout to suppress trap-mediated charge generation. Using the SENSEI detector near the MINOS cavern, we measure a serial-register single-electron density of $(2.9 \pm 0.1) \times 10^{-5}$ electrons/pixel/image under standard SENSEI readout conditions, reduced to $(4.0 \pm 0.4) \times 10^{-6}$ electrons/pixel/image with tri-level clocking -- a factor of $\sim$7 improvement. This technique offers a promising path to lower backgrounds in current and future Skipper-CCD experiments.

### [4] [Singular mean-field limits for fluctuations around equilibrium](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28979)
*Mitia Duerinckx,Pierre-Emmanuel Jabin*

Main category: math.AP

TL;DR: 本文在吉布斯平衡态附近的微扰区域中，研究了具有奇异相互作用的惯性粒子系统的平均场极限，证明了平衡态附近的小涨落渐近地由线性化弗拉索夫方程（linearized Vlasov equation）支配。该结果适用于包括库仑势（Coulomb potential）在内的一类奇异相互作用核，为尚无法获得非线性平均场极限的场景提供了线性化平均场动力学的严格推导。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为具有奇异相互作用的惯性粒子系统（inertial particle systems）在吉布斯平衡（Gibbs equilibrium）附近的动力学行为提供严格的数学描述。由于该场景下的非线性平均场极限（nonlinear mean-field limit）尚未被严格建立，本文旨在通过微扰方法（perturbative regime）先从线性化层面突破这一困难。

Method: 采用微扰方法，将系统视为吉布斯平衡态附近的小涨落（small fluctuations），并通过严格的数学分析证明该涨落的渐近行为由线性化弗拉索夫方程（linearized Vlasov equation）所支配。处理对象涵盖包括库仑情况（Coulomb case）在内的一类广泛奇异相互作用核（singular interaction kernels）。

Result: 证明了在平衡态附近，粒子系统的小涨落在平均场极限下收敛到线性化弗拉索夫方程的解。该结果对维数 d ≤ 3 的库仑相互作用等奇异核均成立。

Conclusion: 本文在非线性平均场极限尚无法建立的情况下，通过微扰分析成功给出了线性化平均场动力学（linearized mean-field dynamics）的严格推导，为理解和处理奇异相互作用系统的平衡态附近行为提供了坚实的理论基础。

Abstract: This work addresses the mean-field limit of inertial particle systems with singular interactions in a perturbative regime around Gibbs equilibrium. We prove that small fluctuations around equilibrium are asymptotically governed by the linearized Vlasov equation. The result applies to a broad class of singular interaction kernels, including the Coulomb case in dimensions $d\le3$. In particular, this provides a rigorous derivation of the linearized mean-field dynamics near equilibrium in settings where the corresponding nonlinear mean-field limit remains out of reach.

</details>
