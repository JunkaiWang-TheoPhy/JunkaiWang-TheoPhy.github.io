### [1] [Searches for Continuous Gravitational Waves from Supernova Remnants in the first part of the LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA Fourth Observing run](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25808)
*The LIGO Scientific collaboration,the Virgo collaboration,the KAGRA collaboration*

Main category: gr-qc

TL;DR: 该研究对15个超新星遗迹进行了连续引力波定向搜索，使用LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA合作组第四观测期前八个月的数据，未发现任何信号，但设定了迄今最严格的应变振幅上限。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究旨在搜索来自15个邻近超新星遗迹（可能包含年轻中子星候选体）的连续引力波（continuous gravitational waves），这些遗迹是引力波探测的重要目标源。

Method: 分析采用五种管道：四种半相干方法——Band-Sampled-Data定向管道、Weave以及两种Viterbi管道（单谐波和双谐波），以及基于互相关的PyStoch管道。这些搜索覆盖宽频带且不假设目标星历的先验知识。

Result: 未发现来自15个源中任何一个的信号证据。在95%置信水平上设定了本征应变振幅上限，对邻近源G266.2-1.2（Vela Jr.）在300 Hz附近达到最严格约束约4×10⁻²⁶。对同一源还推导了中子星椭圆度和r模式振幅的限制。

Conclusion: 这些结果代表了迄今对超新星遗迹连续引力波最灵敏的宽频带定向搜索，为限制中子星物理参数（如椭圆度和r模式振幅）提供了重要约束。

Abstract: We present results from directed searches for continuous gravitational waves from a sample of 15 nearby supernova remnants, likely hosting young neutron star candidates, using data from the first eight months of the fourth observing run (O4) of the LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA Collaboration. The analysis employs five pipelines: four semi-coherent methods -- the Band-Sampled-Data directed pipeline, Weave and two Viterbi pipelines (single- and dual-harmonic) -- and PyStoch, a cross-correlation-based pipeline. These searches cover wide frequency bands and do not assume prior knowledge of the targets' ephemerides. No evidence of a signal is found from any of the 15 sources. We set 95\% confidence-level upper limits on the intrinsic strain amplitude, with the most stringent constraints reaching $\sim 4 \times 10^{-26}$ near 300 Hz for the nearby source G266.2$-$1.2 (Vela Jr.). We also derive limits on neutron star ellipticity and $r$-mode amplitudes for the same source, with the best constraints reaching $\lesssim 10^{-7}$ and $\lesssim 10^{-5}$, respectively, at frequencies above 400 Hz. These results represent the most sensitive wide-band directed searches for continuous gravitational waves from supernova remnants to date.

### [2] [Detectability and Systematic Bias from First-Order Phase-Transition Dephasing in Kerr EMRIs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25897)
*Jingxu Wu,Liangyu Luo,Junyi Zhang,Jiyun Yang,Haoxiang Ma,Jie Shi*

Main category: gr-qc

TL;DR: 该研究分析了克尔极端质量比旋进（Kerr EMRI）中有效一阶相变（first-order phase transition）引起的引力波失相（dephasing）效应，发现尽管波形失配（mismatch）较小，但累积相位差可达数千弧度，表明相变主要影响精度推断的保真度（faithfulness）而非可探测性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究动机在于量化有效一阶相变对克尔极端质量比旋进（Kerr EMRI）引力波信号的观测影响，特别关注相变如何通过耗散通量（dissipative flux）重构产生引力波失相（dephasing），以及这对LISA匹配滤波（matched-filter）诊断的影响。

Method: 采用现象学方法将相变建模为耗散通量（dissipative flux）扇区的有限宽度重构，使用标准LISA匹配滤波（matched-filter）诊断量化观测后果，分析代表性系统（M=2×10⁵M⊙, μ=1.4M⊙, â=0.90）的失配（mismatch）和相位差。

Result: 获得ρ_B=5.064、ρ_T=4.073、ρ_R=1.051等参数，失配（mismatch）为2.986×10⁻³，累积相位差ΔΦ₂₂^SF~5×10³ rad。结果表明窄相变窗口（transition window）能在保持波形全局接近基线分支的同时产生大的相干变形（coherent deformation）。

Conclusion: 相变扇区（transition sector）的主要后果不是可探测性（detectability）损失，而是精度推断的保真度（faithfulness）损失。这促使未来LISA EMRI波形模型将参数化相变扇区直接纳入波形流形（waveform manifold）。

Abstract: We study gravitational-wave dephasing induced by an effective first-order phase transition in a Kerr extreme mass-ratio inspiral (EMRI). The transition is modeled phenomenologically as a finite-width restructuring of the dissipative flux sector, and its observational consequences are quantified with standard LISA matched-filter diagnostics. For a representative system with $M=2\times10^{5}M_\odot$, $μ=1.4M_\odot$, and $\hat a=0.90$, we obtain $ρ_{\rm B}=5.064$, $ρ_{\rm T}=4.073$, $ρ_{\rm R}=1.051$, and a mismatch $\mathcal M=2.986\times10^{-3}$ after maximization over extrinsic time and phase shifts. Although the normalized mismatch remains small, the accumulated phase difference grows to $ΔΦ_{22}^{\rm SF}\sim 5\times10^{3}\,\mathrm{rad}$, indicating that a narrow transition window can generate a large coherent deformation of the inspiral clock while leaving the waveform globally close to the baseline branch in detector-weighted norm. The resulting signal therefore lies in a bias-sensitive regime, characterized by small mismatch, order-unity residual norm, and large cumulative dephasing. Our results suggest that the dominant consequence of the transition sector is not loss of detectability, but loss of faithfulness for precision inference. This motivates future LISA EMRI waveform models that incorporate parameterized transition sectors directly into the waveform manifold.

</details>
